Frequently Asked Questions

What is the MOQ for custom umbrellas?

Low MOQ from 50pcs is available for selected stock-color customization. New materials, special structures or complex packaging may require confirmation.

What logo files should I send?

Vector files such as AI, PDF, EPS or SVG are preferred. If you only have PNG or JPG, send the highest resolution available for review.

Can you customize packaging?

Yes. Common options include fabric sleeve, PP bag, gift box, hang tag, carton mark and brand-ready packaging.

Which umbrella type is best for corporate gifts?

Folding umbrellas and golf umbrellas are the most common choices. Folding umbrellas are portable; golf umbrellas give stronger brand visibility.
